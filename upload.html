How to Append New Data

Please ensure there are no headers in the CSV file.

The data you upload will be added to existing data.

We will expect the CSV to have seven columns of data. The first, fourth, fifth, and sixth columns are important and must be in their respective places as such:

name timestamp x y
Lisa ... ... 4/17/2016 11:17:00 PM 9.454567 -0.428717 ...
etc.
